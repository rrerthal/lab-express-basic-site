  1. Copa Libertadores Final (2019): Gabigol's defining moment came in the 2019 Copa Libertadores final against River Plate. In a dramatic turnaround, he scored two late goals in the final minutes of the match, securing a 2-1 victory for Flamengo and their first Copa Libertadores title in nearly four decades. His last-minute heroics instantly turned him into a legend among Flamengo fans.
  2. Campeonato Brasileiro Performance (2019): Throughout the 2019 Campeonato Brasileiro Série A season, Gabigol's goal-scoring prowess played a pivotal role in helping Flamengo clinch the league title after a decade-long drought. His consistency in finding the back of the net showcased his importance to the team's success.
  3. Campeonato Carioca Final (2020): In the Campeonato Carioca final against Fluminense, Gabigol showcased his penchant for stepping up in crucial moments. He scored both goals in a 2-1 victory for Flamengo, securing yet another state championship and displaying his ability to shine in high-pressure situations.
  4. Record-breaking Goal Against Santos (2021): In February 2021, Gabigol scored his 74th goal for Flamengo in a match against his former club Santos. This goal allowed him to surpass Zico's record as the all-time leading goal scorer in the history of the Maracanã Stadium, a historic achievement that solidified his place in Flamengo's lore.
  5. Supercopa do Brasil (2020): In the 2020 Supercopa do Brasil final against Athletico Paranaense, Gabigol once again demonstrated his knack for decisive moments. He scored both goals in a 3-0 victory, clinching the Supercopa title for Flamengo and showcasing his ability to perform on the big stage.
